Matea Milojkovic

Growing up, many children dream about becoming nurses, police officers, or firefighters. I wanted to be a ninja. Night of the Ninjas— the fifth book in the series The Magic Tree House— sparked my desire to utilize stealth and cunning to bring the “bad guys” to justice. Over the years my career goals have slightly changed and my aspirations have matured: I still look forward to engaging in a fast-paced industry.

I never quite know how to answer the question "Where are you from?" Do I say Serbia and Croatia, where my parents immigranted from? Do I say Vancouver, British Columbia, where I was born? Do I say South Carolina, where I grew up? Because of my background, I truly feel like a global citizen: the world is my backyard and everyone in it my neighbor.
