Parallel Processing

Parallel Processing was a course offered at my university that, due to scheduling, I was unable to take. However, my curiosity in this relevant topic led me to independently dabble in this field. The Parallel Processing professor was helpful in providing me with the course reading materials and was available for questions. My GitHub account contains a repository with the code I've created in my journey of understanding a little bit more of the complexities of parallel computing.

Data Mining

In today's Information Age, data is plentiful but often mysterious. Data Mining and Visualization are vital in order to make sense of and understand data. Under the guidance of two professors (one Computer Science and one Business Analytics), I and another student took an independent study course in Data Mining. I presented the cumulation of our work at the 2018 Symposium on Data Science & Statistics. Our research abstract and presentation can be found on the conference's online program. If you would like a copy of the R Script we used for our analysis, please email me.
